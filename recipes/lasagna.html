Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles(Fresh or dried)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning(blend of basil, oregano, thyme and rosemary)

Optional Ingredients

  • Vegetables such as Mushrooms, bell peppers, zucchini or spinach
  • Herbs such as parsley or basil
  • Red pepper flakes for added spice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add garlic and onion and cook until softened.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and Italian seasoning to the skillet and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Assemble the lasagna in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Start by spreading a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the dish, then add a layer of lasagna noodles. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
