Chicken Pasta Romano

Chicken Pasta Romano is a popular Italian-American dish that typically consists of saut√©ed chicken breast, pasta (usually penne or fettuccine), and a creamy sauce made with heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic, and sometimes sun-dried tomatoes. Some variations of the recipe may include additional ingredients such as mushrooms, spinach, or broccoli.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, diced
  • Penne pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic cloves minced
  • Onion, diced
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Romano cheese, grated
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • Red pepper flakes(optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped(optional)

Optional Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Baby spinach
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the diced chicken breast with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and diced onion. Cook until the onion is translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add the heavy cream to the skillet and stir until heated through.
  6. Add grated Parmesan cheese and Romano cheese to the skillet, stirring constantly until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and add the cooked pasta. Stir until the chicken and pasta are coated in the sauce.
  8. If desired, add cherry tomatoes, baby spinach, or mushrooms to the skillet and cook until heated through.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley and red pepper flakes (if desired).
